Our Story & Mission

My name is Rachel DesRoche, founder of the Rylee Faith Foundation. I founded this charity in memory of my daughter, Rylee Faith, who was born December 2005 at 25 weeks, 3 days gestation (3 1/2 months premature). Rylee Faith was a mere 1lb, 11oz and 12 inches long. She lived in the Ottawa Hospital Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU) for 15 days where she continued to fight for her life. Her life may not have been long, but she left permanent footprints in our lives.

Her short stay in the NICU was over Christmas and New Years, so I knew I wanted to do something for all the NICU babies like her that are fighting for their lives over Christmas, in honour of my angel. The very next Christmas, our family showed up at the Ottawa Hospital NICU on Christmas morning with 30 Christmas Gift Baskets to deliver to each and every NICU baby there. And I have continued to do this every year since.

Every year on Christmas Day we hand deliver 30 Christmas gift baskets to the babies and their families in the Ottawa Hospital NICU. Items include:

  • Giant Teddy Bears
  • Knit blankets, hats, mitts
  • Gift Certificates
  • Diaper Bags
  • Chocolates

These are all purchased with donations or items that have been donated for these baskets. We have had much success from the beginning with donations from large corporations such as Wal-Mart, Tim Hortons, Nestle Baby; local businesses; friends; family; and strangers who all support this.
